About Our Parish

Saint Peter Claver Church was established in 1931 to serve the African American community of greater Tyler, Texas. Today, the parish is a trilingual community with Masses in English and Spanish.

Our doors are open to all through various ministries and services that witness to the love of God in our neighborhood and beyond.

Explore our ministries

Our Mission

We are one spiritual community called to follow Jesus Christ and to share the Good News in word and deed, rooted in prayer, worship, and service.
